About

This website was created to allow easy access to statistics and data about riders, events and standings. There are multiple filtering and sorting options for browsing data as well as search bar for fast and precise searching.

Most of the data is dependent on official FMB World Tour website. However on this website you will also be able to find riders age, sex, personal descriptions and points for the runs in particular events. On every rider or event page there is sources section with listed references.

RideFMB is a non-profit project. It was coded with Django. If you spot any bugs or you believe that we violate any copyright or you just see some space for development feel free to contact us using our contact form.
